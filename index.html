Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I join a volunteer project?
A: Go to the "Projects" section and click "View Project" to learn more and participate.

Q: Do I need to register?

A: No registration is needed. Just choose a project and follow the instructions provided.

Q: Is there any cost to volunteer?

A: No, all volunteer opportunities listed on our platform are free of charge.

Q: Can I contact project coordinators?

A: Yes, each project includes contact information for the coordinator.
